Wild & Scenic Raft Trips

A guided Wild and Scenic Raft Trip down the Snake Riveris by far one of the top activities not to miss! For ten miles along the Snake River, out guides will show you amazing Teton views, share in-depth knowledge about the local flore and fauna, and often yo'll get a close-up view of wildlife, including bald eagle, moose, bison, else, osprey, and beavers.

Lunch or Dinner Trips
To make your Wild and Scenic Raft Trip even more memorable, try our lunch or dinner Snake River trips -- where we stop along the way at a private meal site at the Snake River OVerlook. This unique Western cookout includes a delicious lunch of flame-grilled hamburgers and hotdogs and for dinner local favorites such as fresh river trout and chrbroiled steaks. Lunch Trips are scheduled daily and depart at 9:00 am or 11:30 am. Dinner Trips are schedules on Tuesday, Thurrsday, and Saturday at 4:30pm. Price include round trip transportation.
